Carnitas Recipe

Sophia's infamous tacos with slow cooked pork and pickled onions.

Ingredients

For the meat

  • 3 lb. skinless, boneless pork shoulder (Boston Butt), cut into 2" pieces
  • 2 jalapeños, cut in half lengthwise, stems removed
  • 4 large garlic cloves
  • 1 Tbsp. Diamond Crystal or 1¼ tsp. Morton kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp. ground coriander
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin

Instructions for meat

  1. place pork, jalapeños, garlic, salt, oregano, pepper, coriander, and cumin in Instant Pot. Toss with your hands until well combined. Seal lid and cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  2. release pressure manually. Using a slotted spoon, transfer pork to a large bowl; fish out jalapeños and garlic and discard. Shred meat with two forks or your hands. Pour cooking liquid over meat and toss to combine.
  3. working in batches, transfer shredded pork to a large nonstick skillet set over high heat and cook, undisturbed, until underside is well browned, about 5 minutes. Toss and continue to cook, tossing occasionally, until some of the edges are caramelized and crisp, 5–7 minutes longer. Using a slotted spoon, transfer to a paper towel–lined baking sheet. Cover with foil to keep warm and moist.

For the pickled onions

  • 2 red onions (thinly sliced)
  • 8-10 habanero peppers (thinly sliced)
  • 3/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1.5 cup water
  • 4 tbsp (1/4 cup) soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp sugar

Instructions for onions

  • place sliced red onions and habaneros into wide mouth mason jar, or any storage container
  • in a separate bowl mix apple cider vinegar, water, soy sauce, and sugar until dissolved
  • pour liquid into mason jar to cover red onions and habaneros
  • let sit for at least 30 minutes before serving
